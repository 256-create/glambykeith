Where Heritage Meets High Glamour

Unveiling the Most Radiant Version of You

I am Agaba Keith

For over 5 years, I've been passionate about using makeup not to transform, but to reveal. My philosophy is rooted in enhancing your unique features and skin's natural luminosity, ensuring you look and feel impeccably like you—just amplified.

Born and raised in Uganda's vibrant creative landscape, I founded Glam by Keith with a mission: to put Ugandan beauty artistry on the global map while celebrating the distinct beauty of African features.

Specializing in soft glam, bridal elegance, and transformative artistry, I believe makeup is armor and self-expression—a tool to celebrate your most important moments with confidence and grace.

My work is a celebration of African beauty in its most refined form—from the intricate elegance of traditional bridal glamour to the contemporary edge of a fashion-forward transformation. This dedication has cultivated a prestigious clientele who trust in a standard of quality, discretion, and unparalleled results.
