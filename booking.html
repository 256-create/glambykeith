Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
For bridal services, we recommend booking 6-9 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (June-December). For events, 3-4 weeks is usually sufficient, but earlier is always better to secure your date.
Do you travel for weddings and events?
Yes! I am available for travel across Uganda and East Africa. Travel fees are calculated based on location and accommodation requirements. Destination wedding inquiries are welcomed and handled with special attention.
What products do you use?
I use only luxury, professional-grade brands suitable for all skin types and specifically selected for longevity in Uganda's climate. All products are sanitized between clients, and I prioritize high-quality formulas that photograph beautifully.
Do you offer trials for bridal makeup?
Yes, all bridal packages include a trial session. This is an essential step to perfect your look, test longevity, and ensure we're both aligned on the vision for your special day.
What is your cancellation policy?
A 50% deposit is required to secure your date. Full refund is available for cancellations made 90 days prior to the event. For cancellations within 90 days, the deposit may be applied to a future date or service, subject to availability.
